The MCRI runs completely on generous donations. Every contribution — whether $50 or $50,000 — directly changes the trajectory of a student's life. You can fund a scholarship for a young woman's secondary education, or support the operating costs of the program itself.

In Zimbabwe, families pay for the equivalent of Junior and Senior year of high school. A donation of $600–$1,000 covers a young girl's school fees for an entire year — making the path to the MCRI possible.
