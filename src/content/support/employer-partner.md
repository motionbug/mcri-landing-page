If you want to make the biggest impact on the lives of others, becoming an employer partner is the way to do it. As an employer partner, you commit to taking on an MCRI apprentice — and potentially hiring them full-time. This means coming to Victoria Falls, meeting students in person, and investing real trust in a young Zimbabwean professional.

Our employer partners tell us that within weeks, their MCRI apprentice is contributing meaningfully to their team. The quality of preparation is that high.

Download our Employment Partner Brief for the full details on how the partnership works, what we ask of you, and what you get in return.
